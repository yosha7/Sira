The Friction

Solving the daily hassle of mechanical airflow control.

Sleep Discomfort

Waking up freezing or sweating disrupts deep REM sleep cycles. The room temperature drops, but your fan speed doesn't.

Smart Home Barriers

Traditional smart switches require electricians, rewiring, and are entirely useless to renters who can't modify the walls.

Accessibility Limits

Standard wall regulators are often out of reach or difficult to turn for the elderly, children, or those with mobility issues.

Business Scope & Scale

A highly scalable hardware model designed for the Indian retrofit market.

The Massive Market Gap

Millions of households rely on analog ceiling fans. Current smart home solutions demand expensive smart switches, requiring electricians and rewiring. Sira bypasses this barrier entirely by offering a 10-second physical retrofit, instantly opening up a massive, previously inaccessible demographic with high hardware margins.

The Renter's Advantage

Renters cannot alter electrical wiring in their apartments. Sira is 100% removable. When a user moves out, they simply unclip Sira and take it with them.

B2B Hospitality Scope

Beyond D2C, Sira is targeting hotels and student hostels. These institutions want energy savings via automation, but cannot afford to rewire 500+ rooms. Sira offers a zero-downtime integration.
